Our Story

Welcome to The Pink Coffee Corner, your cozy home away from home right in the heart of Melbourne. Born out of a passion for smooth, premium lattes and vibrant community spaces, we pride ourselves on serving up the best coffee in town alongside a welcoming smile. Whether you are catching up with friends, settling in with a smooth flat white to get some work done using our free Wi-Fi, or dropping by for lunch, our vibrant little corner is designed to be your perfect daily escape.

At The Pink Coffee Corner, we believe that incredible cafe experiences should be for everyone. That’s why we’ve carefully crafted a diverse, delicious menu packed with premium specialty drinks, fresh smoothies, and decadent treats. From our crowd-favorite chocolate cupcakes and donuts to rich cheesecakes, we intentionally offer a wide variety of vegan, gluten-free, and dietary-friendly options. We don't compromise on flavor, so you can always indulge safely and happily.
